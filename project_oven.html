Thermal Analysis of Oven

Project for:
Role FEA Analyst and Teaching Assitant
Simulation Type Thermo-Mechanical Simulation
Software Used
Siemens NX Siemens Simcenter

Objective

To develop a comprehensive tutorial guiding engineers through the thermal analysis of an oven, focusing on accurate prediction of temperature distribution, heat transfer, and resulting thermal stresses.

Contribution

  • Created detailed CAD models of the oven components using Siemens NX to accurately represent geometry relevant for thermal analysis.
  • Conducted coupled thermal and structural simulations in Siemens Simcenter, incorporating conduction, convection, and radiation heat transfer phenomena.
  • Analyzed temperature gradients and thermal stresses under various operating conditions to assess material performance and potential failure points.
  • Developed a step-by-step tutorial based on the thermal analysis methodology, facilitating standardization of simulation approaches and knowledge transfer within Siemens engineering teams.

Results

Predicted critical temperature distributions and thermal stresses in oven components through coupled simulations, enabling design improvements and providing a reusable Siemens tutorial to standardize thermal analysis practices.

Key Achievements:

  • Predicted thermal profiles and mechanical stresses in oven components, enabling proactive design improvements.
  • Guided optimizations to enhance heat distribution and reduce fatigue risk.
  • Provided a reusable tutorial and workflow, accelerating future thermal analyses and improving prediction reliability.
