Development of a Reusable, Custom 3D-Printable Mask Using Facial Scanning Technology

Project for:
Role Project Lead, Team Lead, Design Engineer, and FEA Analyst
Simulation Type CAD Modeling and Mechanical/Structural Analysis
Software Used
Siemens NX Siemens Simcenter

Objective

To design and develop a reusable, customized 3D-printable face mask using facial scanning technology to address mask shortages and enhance user safety during airborne health crises.

Contribution

  • Led a multidisciplinary student team, delegating tasks related to design, simulation, and testing to ensure project alignment and timely delivery.
  • Directed the overall project strategy, ensuring coordination between design, materials, and analysis teams.
  • Led the design and engineering of a reusable 3D-printable face mask optimized for comfort, fit, and protection against airborne pathogens.
  • Designed a three-part modular mask system incorporating a custom gasket to ensure a secure facial seal and prevent air leakage.
  • Supported material selection and specification to ensure safety, durability, and compatibility with standard 3D printing processes.
  • Conducted structural and functional analyses using Finite Element Analysis (FEA) to validate performance under real-world conditions.

Results

Successfully developed a cost-effective, reusable 3D-printable mask customized through facial scanning, validated through structural simulations, and recognized in multiple media outlets for its innovation and social impact.
